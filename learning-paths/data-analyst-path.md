# 📊 Data Analyst Path — Excel for Analysis

**Level:** Intermediate | **Challenges:** 5 | **Total Time:** ~140–195 minutes

This path is designed for **aspiring data analysts** who want to use Excel professionally. You'll cover the full data workflow: clean → look up → summarize → visualize.

---

## Your Journey

### Step 1 — [Duplicate Detective](../challenges/data-cleaning/duplicate-detective/)
**Difficulty:** 🟢 Apprentice | **Time:** 20–30 min

Every data analyst's first job is to trust the data. Learn to identify and remove duplicates before any analysis begins.

**Skills:** `COUNTIF`, conditional formatting, Remove Duplicates

---

### Step 2 — [VLOOKUP Treasure Hunt](../challenges/formulas/vlookup-treasure-hunt/)
**Difficulty:** 🟢 Apprentice | **Time:** 20–30 min

Join datasets together using the most well-known Excel lookup function.

**Skills:** `VLOOKUP`, `IFERROR`, exact match

---

### Step 3 — [INDEX-MATCH Maze](../challenges/formulas/index-match-maze/)
**Difficulty:** 🔵 Journeyman | **Time:** 30–45 min

Upgrade your lookup game. INDEX-MATCH is more powerful and flexible than VLOOKUP, and it's what professionals use.

**Skills:** `INDEX`, `MATCH`, left-lookup, two-way lookup

---

### Step 4 — [Pivot Pyramid](../challenges/pivot-tables/pivot-pyramid/)
**Difficulty:** 🔵 Journeyman | **Time:** 30–45 min

Turn raw data into meaningful summaries with Pivot Tables — a core skill for any data analyst.

**Skills:** Pivot Tables, grouping, slicers, calculated fields

---

### Step 5 — [Dashboard Duel](../challenges/data-analysis/dashboard-duel/)
**Difficulty:** 🔵 Journeyman | **Time:** 40–60 min

Bring your analysis to life. Build a complete Excel dashboard with charts, heatmaps, and sparklines.

**Skills:** Charts, conditional formatting, sparklines, named ranges

---
